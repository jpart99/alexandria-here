## The 60-second evidence path

1. Open the [live product](https://alexandria-here.cinemaexile.chatgpt.site).
2. Browse the [corrected witnessed recovery](https://alexandria-here.cinemaexile.chatgpt.site/r/18026989-33be-4011-86ee-19e1754cb22c), then turn on **Show the Seams**.
3. Open its [machine Recovery Receipt](https://alexandria-here.cinemaexile.chatgpt.site/api/recover/18026989-33be-4011-86ee-19e1754cb22c/receipt).
4. In `decisions`, confirm `era_selection.proposedBy` is `deterministic`, `page_order.proposedBy` is `gpt-5.6`, and every `primary_witness.proposedBy` is `gpt-5.6`.
5. Confirm every `validationResults[].passed` is `true`.

Bottom line: this proof records a live bounded recovery. GPT-5.6 made two decision types—page ordering and one primary-witness choice per returned preserved page—while deterministic code fixed the temporal window, validated IDs, and rendered historical body text only from persisted archive extracts. The receipt proves internal provenance and consistency for this run, not historical truth, rights clearance, independent model attestation, or universal recovery.

## Explicit non-claims

- A successful receipt evidences one bounded run, not universal recovery or identical replay.
- Candidate windows show bounded comparison, not comprehensive history or multiple returned editions.
- Passing validators establish the checks they name, not independent historical verification or absence of software defects.
- Public source and tests establish inspectable implementation and reproducible checks, not a formal proof that the hosted binary is byte-identical to source.
- The Devpost draft, contest track, credits, and prepared media do not establish final submission, eligibility, acceptance, judging, or OpenAI endorsement.

```bash
npm run build
npm audit --omit=dev --audit-level=moderate
```
