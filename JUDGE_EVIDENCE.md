1. Open the [live product](https://alexandria-here.cinemaexile.chatgpt.site).

4. In `decisions`, confirm `era_selection.proposedBy` is `deterministic`, `page_order.proposedBy` is `gpt-5.6`, and every `primary_witness.proposedBy` is `gpt-5.6`.
5. Confirm every `validationResults[].passed` is `true`.

Bottom line: this proof records a live bounded recovery. GPT-5.6 made two decision types—page ordering and one primary-witness choice per returned preserved page—while deterministic code fixed the temporal window, validated IDs, and rendered historical body text only from persisted archive extracts. The receipt records provenance and named internal-consistency results for this run; it does not prove historical truth, rights clearance, independent model attestation, or universal recovery.

## Explicit non-claims

- A successful receipt evidences one bounded run, not universal recovery, a standalone replay package, or an identical archive-dependent rerun.
- Candidate windows show bounded comparison, not comprehensive history or multiple returned editions.
- Passing validators establish the checks they name, not independent historical verification or absence of software defects.
- Public source and tests establish inspectable implementation and reproducible checks, not a formal proof that the hosted binary is byte-identical to source.
